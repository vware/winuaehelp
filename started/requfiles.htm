As mentioned in the Legal Notes, you can not use WinUAE without proprietary Amiga software.
In the "Amiga Programs" subdirectory you will find two small Amiga programs that will help you generate the necessary image files. NEVER run either of these programs from the Workbench:

  • transdisk - Transfer floppy disks, i.e. Workbench
  • transrom - Transfer Kickstart ROM data
